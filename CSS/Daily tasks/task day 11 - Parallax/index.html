It contains ultra-low levels of particle densities, constituting a near-perfect vacuum of predominantly hydrogen and helium plasma, permeated by electromagnetic radiation, cosmic rays, neutrinos, magnetic fields and dust. The Solar System is a remarkable cosmic neighborhood teeming with diversity and beauty. At its heart lies the Sun, a blazing star that provides the energy sustaining life on Earth. Encircling this fiery center are eight unique planets, each with its own set of wonders. Our own Earth, the third planet, stands out as a blue oasis, home to water, life, and ever-changing climates.
